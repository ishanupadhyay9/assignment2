Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar – Built by Maharaja Jai Singh II in 1734, this UNESCO-listed observatory houses 19 astronomical instruments, including the world’s largest stone sundial. It showcases Jaipur’s advanced scientific knowledge in astronomy and timekeeping.

The fort offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes and showcases the rich military history of Rajasthan. Its underground water storage system and armory museum are notable attractions. Jaigarh Fort remains a testament to the strategic and architectural prowess of the Rajputs.
